**Product Thinking in Practice**

I’m a technical product manager focused on building data-driven products that solve real business problems. This repository reflects how I approach product development: with clarity of purpose, strong alignment between stakeholders and engineering, and a bias toward delivering measurable value.

My work sits at the intersection of product strategy, data platforms, and user experience. I specialize in translating complex business needs into scalable, well-defined solutions, particularly in environments involving data products, analytics, and modern cloud architectures.

**What You’ll See Here
**Structured approaches to product discovery and prioritization
Lightweight tools and prototypes that support decision-making
Examples of how I think about user needs, tradeoffs, and outcomes
Practical applications of agile and iterative development principles

**How I Work**
Start with the problem, not the solution
Validate assumptions early through discovery and stakeholder input
Prioritize based on impact, feasibility, and strategic alignment
Partner closely with engineering and data teams to deliver effectively
Measure success through adoption, usability, and business outcomes

**Areas of Focus**
Data products and analytics platforms
AI-enabled decision support and insights
Supply chain and operational systems
Developer and internal tooling experiences

This repo is a working space for ideas, experiments, and tools that reflect my approach to product management in real-world environments.
